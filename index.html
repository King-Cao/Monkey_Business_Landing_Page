Our Story

We started as a humble banana farm.

However we realized that for every dozen banana's farmed, there is a loss of one banana tree. In the long run, this goes up to hundreds of banana tree's lost every year! We have developed state of the art technology that allows us to take our extra banana tree's and turn them into business suits. We will then use part of the revenue from every suit sold to plant a banana tree. Each tree has the ability to house up to tens of monkey's!

Suit up monkey's, because we mean business!
